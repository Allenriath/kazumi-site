Frequent Asked Questions !


  • Can you draw for free?

    • Do you work for free? Me neither.

  • What are the payment methods?

  • When you will start the work ?

    • Once I receive the payment (or half of it, according with the agreement).

  • Which programs do you use ?

    • Paint Tool SAI and Photoshop

  • How many drawings can I request ?

    • As many as you wish! But of course, we have to talk about it if is a big project.

  • When will you start?

    • I will start the request once the first half of the payment is received

  • How long it takes to be done ?

    • It depends of how much busy I'll be and how many detail it will have. (I also have a life beyond may drawings, you know?). And most of all, it will depend on the details level of the request. The deadline will be set at the time of the request, but I have a human life after all. Bad things can happen to me, such as insomnia, I can get sick, I can be mugged, a dog can bite my hand, I can get shot by a stranger, a bus full of Mexicans can lose control and destroy my house or I can simply be taken back home by some UFO.

  • I need to change some details on my request! Can I do this?

    • I will send you WIP screenshots gradually so if there's something you want to add/modify you can just ask.

  • Which kind of drawing can I request?

    • Any kind you wish. Hentai, yaoi, yuri, romance, nudity, furry, gore, etc... Keep in mind that there are certain types that still limited for me, like the furry exemple above. But it will be discussed directly with the customer. I do not feel discomfort with this kind of theme. Probably, I will only refuse a request in VERY extremes cases. Like a drawing for a campaign that supports something criminal for example.

  • Can I use your drawing in a book, shirt, etc?

    • This is a VERY delicate subject. In the case of PERSONAL USE, yes, you can do. Requesting a drawing for a shirt that YOU'RE going to use is ok (or to give as a gift, to use it like banner on your channel, fanpage, whatever). This is something of personal use, without lucrative endings. But if you wish to reproduce for large-scale sales for example, you need to remember that you DO NOT have rights over the image, so we need to discuss it before, cause there's a copyright tax that varies accordingly with the demand. When I started in this area I had no knowledge of laws and how people could take advantage of me, but I'm older and safer now, so we will need to sit and discuss your intentions with my work. The values for copyrights must be paid beforehand in the case of large-scale production and lucrative endings!

  • How can I contact you?

    • If it's about comission requests, please send a email to kazumitakashi@yahoo.com
    Otherwise, just use my social networks. I would say that Twitter may be your best bet.
